Enroll

Enrollment at Ob Montessori Center opens the door to a learning environment shaped by independence, curiosity, and meaningful growth. Families can expect a guided admissions experience that helps determine the best path for each learner. The school welcomes students who thrive in hands-on exploration and an environment designed to support both academic and personal development. Each application is reviewed thoughtfully to ensure a strong fit between student, family, and community.
